FAQs

How is this different to a job board?

Aren't you tired of sending your CV to different companies all the time? With us, you don't have to go through that trouble. You just create one profile. We will match you to open positions that are posted by our employers directly - automatically.

How will employers contact me?

Once employers decide they want to consider you for an interview, they will tell us. We will e-mail you and ask for your permission to give out your contact details. If you grant it, we will send your profile to the employer.

How will I get matched for jobs?

Our recommendation engine will look at all the information that you have entered in your profile. It will then match this information against the jobs already posted. When we make a match, we tell the employer.

Is my data secure?

We take your privacy seriously. We use industry-standard IT infrastructure. Employers will by default see an anonymous version of your profile, and they will contact you when they need access to your full profile.

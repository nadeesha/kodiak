How do I get paid for referring my friends?

Step 1: You generate your own unique referral url and send it to friends

Click on the "Tell a friend" link on any advertisement and we will generate you a unique web address that you can send to your friends on email, chat, facebook or any other way.

Step 2: Your friend signs up and applies to the advertisement through hirewire

Once your friends click the link, they will see the advertisement. If they choose to apply, we will record your details along with your friend's application.

Step 3: If employer selects your friend, we will contact you and give you the money

Once the employer recieves the application, and if your friend gets hired, they will let us know, and we will contact you to send your prize money.
